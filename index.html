About US:

The Islamia University of Bahawalpur is a public sector university in Bahawalpur, Pakistan, which was originally established by His Highness Nawab Sir Sadiq Muhammad Khan Abbasi - V as the Jamia Abbasia in 1925. To align with the modern needs and changing world, recently, the number of department has been increased from 26 to 129, offering more than 300 disciplines. At present around 1800 teachers including 550 PhDs are serving to provide latest and quality education. Apart from conventional disciplines of religious affairs, languages, social and basic sciences, is being imparted in the areas of Allied Health Innovative, Understanding and being Beneficial.
